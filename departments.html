Department and Units

Use your gifts to build the kingdom

Prayer

We pray and intercede for the church and all our members. Our mission is to pray and never stop praying. We pray and testimonies abound.

Choir

We use our talents and voices to lead the church to worship and praise God.

Ushering

We create conducive atmosphere for services. We are all things to all men. Finding and meeting every need.

Protocol

We maintain etiquette and orderliness around the altar. We coordinate time management during services and protocolling of guest ministers.

Evangelism

We have passion to reach out to lost souls. We lead the church to preach the word of God to everyone. We map out the community for effective evangelism.

ICT / Media

We create beautiful service experience by producing quality video and audio for all our services. We are responsible for the live streaming of all our services and managing social media platforms of the church.

Children / Teens

We teach and train our young ones in the way of the Lord.

Drama

We bring the word of God to manifestation by acting. We create the visual format of the Word of God.
