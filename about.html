About RCCG Jesus House, Minna

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Jesus House, Minna was planted 1st of July 2007, later became the Provincial Headquarters of RCCG Niger 1 in 2010, and metamorphosised into Region 41 Headquarters in 2022. This is a family church where the presence of God is evident and a home where everyone is important as you are important to God.

RCCG VISION & MISSION:

  • ​To make heaven
  • To take as many people as possible with us
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations
  • To accomplish number 1, holiness will be our lifestyle
  • We will plant churches on every street in every city, town, and community in all nations of the world
  • ​We will pursue these objectives until every nation in the world is reached for JESUS CHRIST OUR LORD

RCCG MOTTO

Jesus Christ the same yesterday, and today, and for ever. (Hebrew 13:8)

Our Units and Departments

Prayer
We pray and intercede for the church and all our members. Our mission is to pray and never stop praying. We pray and testimonies abound.
Choir
We use our talents and voices to lead the church to worship and praise God.
Ushering
We create conducive atmosphere for services. We are all things to all men. Finding and meeting every need.
Protocol
We maintain etiquette and orderliness around the altar. We coordinate time management during services and protocolling of guest ministers.
Evangelism
We have passion to reach out to lost souls. We lead the church to preach the word of God to everyone. We map out the community for effective evangelism.
ICT / Media
We create beautiful service experience by producing quality video and audio for all our services. We are responsible for the live streaming of all our services and managing social media platforms of the church.
Children / Teens
We teach and train our young ones in the way of the Lord.
Drama
We bring the word of God to manifestation by acting. We create the visual format of the Word of God.
Follow-up / Counselling
We are responsible for our guests and new members. We collate the data base of our members and reach out to them from time to time
Sunday School
We teach the undiluted Word of God using the church manual as guide.
Welfare
We look after the wellbeing of church members. Also, we reach out to the less privileged in the community
Redeemed Volunteers
We are responsible for orderliness of traffic around the church premises. The security of lives and properties is paramount to us.
Medical
We provide basic first aid care to members in case of emergency. Also, we meet medical needs of the people in the community during outreaches.
Technical
We see to proper connections of electrical and sound hardware.
