About Us

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Jesus House, Minna was planted 1st of July 2007, later became the Provincial Headquarters of RCCG Niger 1 in 2010, and metamorphosised into Region 41 Headquarters in 2022. This is a family church where the presence of God is evident and a home where everyone is important as you are important to God.

You are not just a number; you count in Jesus House.
